Grok AI Telegram Bot Pricing and Free Quota

Cost model is designed for casual and heavy users: a free monthly quota plus optional pay-as-you-go top-ups.

Current model

Plan What is included Best for
Free 5 Quick requests per day after channel subscription. Occasional use and testing.
Quick mode cost 1 token per request. Daily chats and lightweight tasks.
Stars top-up Pay-as-you-go tokens purchased via Telegram Stars. Variable usage without monthly commitment.

Top-up packs

Current packs from the bot payment panel:

  • 75 Stars -> 100 tokens
  • 150 Stars -> 200 tokens
  • 225 Stars -> 300 tokens
  • 375 Stars -> 500 tokens
  • 750 Stars -> 1000 tokens
  • 1500 Stars -> 2000 tokens

When pay-as-you-go is cheaper

  • You ask a few high-value questions per week, not every day.
  • Your usage is seasonal (work sprints, exam prep, launches).
  • You want cost control per request instead of fixed monthly spend.
