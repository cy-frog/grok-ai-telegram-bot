Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this an official Grok Telegram bot?

No. It is a community bot that provides Grok access inside Telegram.

2. Is there a free tier?

Yes. Subscribers currently get 5 free Quick requests each day.

3. What are Telegram Stars?

Stars are Telegram in-app credits used for top-ups and digital purchases.

4. Why did the bot stop replying?

Common reasons: temporary API load, quota reached, Telegram delivery issues, or malformed request.

5. Can I use languages other than English?

Yes. The bot supports 11 interface languages and multilingual prompts.

6. Does it remember previous messages?

Only the last 2 messages are kept as temporary context for continuity.

7. Do you store my entire chats?

No long-term full message history database is maintained for normal usage.

8. What account data is stored?

User/account identifier, balance, quota state, and subscription/payment status.

9. Is pay-as-you-go cheaper than subscription?

For irregular usage, often yes. For heavy daily usage, compare effective per-request cost.

10. How much does one Quick request cost?

Quick mode currently costs 1 token per request.

11. What top-up packs are available?

Current packs include 100/200/300/500/1000/2000 tokens via Stars.

12. What does New Chat do?

It resets dialogue context, so Grok stops using previous messages in that thread.

13. Which modes are available?

Quick, Smart, Internet, and Images. Paid modes can have separate limits or pricing.

14. Are replies plain text only?

No. The bot supports richer response formatting, including cleaner structured output.

15. Are refunds available?

Refund handling depends on platform/payment rules and support review.

16. Is there a strict rate limit?

Yes, limits can apply to protect service quality and prevent abuse spikes.

17. Why does Google show different pages in results?

Search engines choose pages based on relevance, authority, and crawl/index signals over time.

18. Is this a "ChatGPT killer"?

No. It is a practical Telegram AI bot option for users who want Grok access inside Telegram.

19. Can I find a free Grok Telegram bot here?

Yes. This site documents free daily usage and paid top-up options for the community Grok AI Telegram bot.

20. Is this page about a working Grok Telegram setup?

Yes. Check Status for current working modes, quotas, and availability notes.
